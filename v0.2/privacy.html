Privacy

Effective date: March 13, 2026

TaskLeaders is an MVP. We keep data collection minimal and focused on operating the service. We do not run ad targeting or build an identity profile of you.

1) What we collect

  • Application submissions (Become a TaskLeader): name, email, WhatsApp number, service area, and a short description.
  • Basic connection event logs: when someone opens the Connect modal and when they attempt to submit a Connect request.
  • Standard technical data: your browser may automatically send basic information (e.g., user agent). We may store limited technical metadata to debug issues.

2) What our connection event logs mean (plain MVP language)

When you interact with the Connect flow, we may record a small event so we can understand if the flow is working and improve reliability. These events use a random session identifier stored in your browser. We are not using this for ad targeting.

3) How we use data

  • To operate the MVP (review applications, keep the site working).
  • To improve the Connect experience and reduce errors.

4) WhatsApp is a third-party service

If you choose to connect via WhatsApp, your messages and usage are governed by WhatsApp’s terms and privacy policy.

5) What we do not do

  • We do not sell your personal information.
  • We do not run targeted advertising based on your activity.

6) Retention

We retain data only as long as reasonably needed to operate the MVP, review applications, and troubleshoot issues.

7) Contact / deletion requests

If you want to request deletion of an application submission, contact: privacy@taskleaders.example

8) Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as the MVP evolves. The effective date above indicates the latest version.
