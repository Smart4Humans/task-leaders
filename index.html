Why I'm building this

Finding good local help shouldn't be this hard.

I'm Todd Brunel, and I started TaskLeaders because finding reliable local help should be faster, clearer, and less random.

People looking for help should not have to sort through ads, scattered reviews, vague websites, and slow replies. At the same time, strong local service pros should not be buried beside businesses that simply paid more or looked louder online.

TaskLeaders is being built to connect people who need fast, reliable help with strong local service pros in their area, starting with the categories and communities where trust matters most. Realtors and property managers, who hire again and again, are part of that demand from the start.

Good local pros deserve to stand out for the right reasons, and good clients deserve a clearer way to find them.

Todd Brunel
TaskLeaders Network Inc.

Good questions

Straight answers.

What is the TaskLeaders Marketplace?
The TaskLeaders Marketplace is being built to help people find fast, reliable local help from service pros who meet TaskLeaders standards. TaskLeaders is currently onboarding top service pros in select categories before opening more broadly.
What is my TaskLeaders Score used for?
Your TaskLeaders Score helps you understand how your business appears against other local service pros in your category. It looks at public reputation, review volume, trust signals, online footprint, and responsiveness. Your score is private, not a public ranking, and may be used as one input when TaskLeaders reviews Marketplace eligibility.
Is my TaskLeaders Score public?
No. Your assessment is private. TaskLeaders does not publish your score, rank, or position on a public leaderboard.
When is the TaskLeaders Marketplace launching?
The Marketplace will open category by category as TaskLeaders builds enough qualified local service pros to create a useful client experience.
Does a free assessment guarantee Marketplace access?
No. A free assessment does not guarantee Marketplace invitation or visibility. Marketplace participation depends on TaskLeaders standards, category fit, service area, and network needs.
