# Task Leaders — Zero-Cost Provider Outreach Plan

**Goal:** 50 founding providers in 30 days  
**Budget:** $0 (time only)  
**Target:** Handymen, plumbers, electricians, painters, cleaners in Vancouver

---

## Phase 1: Direct Outreach (Week 1-2)

### Craigslist — Free, High Intent

**Where:** Vancouver > Services > Skilled Trade

**Strategy:**
1. Search "handyman", "plumber", "electrician", "painter", "cleaner"
2. Find ads with phone numbers (WhatsApp-ready)
3. Send 20 messages/day

**Message Template:**
```
Hi [Name], saw your ad on Craigslist. I'm building a new 
service marketplace in Vancouver that ranks providers by 
response speed — like a leaderboard. 

We're looking for 50 founding providers to join free. 
First 5 customers are on us. No catch, no credit card.

Interested in a 5-min WhatsApp call to learn more?

Todd
Task Leaders
```

**Time:** 1 hour/day → 20 providers contacted/day

---

### Facebook Groups — Free, Local, Trust-Based

**Groups to Join:**
- Vancouver Handyman Services
- Vancouver Trades & Services
- North Shore Moms (ask for referrals)
- Vancouver Small Business Network
- Local community groups (Kitsilano, Mount Pleasant, etc.)

**Strategy:**
1. Join as yourself (not as "Task Leaders")
2. Lurk for 2-3 days, comment helpfully
3. Post:

**Post Template:**
```
👋 Vancouver service providers!

I'm building something new — a ranked marketplace where 
customers find you based on how fast you respond.

Looking for 50 founding providers:
✓ Handymen
✓ Plumbers  
✓ Electricians
✓ Painters
✓ Cleaners

What's different:
• First 5 customers FREE (no catch)
• You keep 100% of earnings
• We rank you by speed, not who pays most

I'm vetting each provider personally with a quick video call.

If you're great at what you do and want more customers, 
DM me or comment below.

Todd
```

**Time:** 30 min/day engagement + 1 post/week

---

### Nextdoor — Free, Hyper-Local

**Strategy:**
1. Create personal account (not business)
2. Post in "Recommendations":

**Post:**
```
Looking for great local service providers!

I'm building a platform to help neighbors find reliable 
handymen, plumbers, electricians, etc. ranked by how 
fast they respond.

If you know someone great (or you are someone great!), 
I'd love to chat. Vetting each person personally.

Thanks!
Todd
```

**Time:** 15 min/day responding to comments

## Phase 2: Referral Engine (Week 2-4)

### Provider Referrals — $0 Cost, High Trust

**Every signup ask:**
> "Know 2 other great providers? Send them my way and 
> I'll prioritize their application."

**Incentive:** None needed early — social proof + helping friends

**Later incentive:** $50 account credit for each referred provider who subscribes

---

### Customer Referrals — Future Providers

**On every customer inquiry:**
> "Know a great [category] who should be on Task Leaders?"

---

## Red Flags (Don't Approve)

- No tools or can't show workspace
- Can't explain their work clearly
- Pushy about getting listed immediately
- No references or portfolio
- Evasive about experience

---

## Green Flags (Fast-Track)

- Shows tools/workspace enthusiastically
- Has photos of past work
- Other customers can vouch for them
- Asks smart questions about the platform
- Responsive to messages
